Community Garden Website Plan

Site Name

GrowTogether Community Garden

This name was selected because it emphasizes both the agricultural aspect ("Grow") and the community involvement ("Together") that are central to our mission. The name is memorable, positive, and clearly communicates the purpose of the organization. Potential domain: growtogether-garden.org

Site Purpose

The GrowTogether Community Garden website serves as a digital hub for our local community garden, providing essential information and services to garden members, volunteers, and the wider community. The site facilitates plot management, event coordination, volunteer organization, and educational resources, while promoting sustainable living and community engagement.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

"I'm interested in getting a garden plot - how do I find out if there are any available, what the requirements are, and how to apply?"

Scenario 2:

"I want to volunteer at the garden but I don't know what opportunities are available or how to sign up. Where can I find this information?"

Scenario 3:

"When is the next community workshop and what topics will be covered? I'm particularly interested in learning about composting."

Color Schema

Primary Green (#2E7D32): Used for headings and primary buttons

Secondary Brown (#795548): Used for accents and secondary elements

Background Light (#F1F8E9): Used for page backgrounds

Text Dark (#1B5E20): Used for body text

Typography

Primary Font: Montserrat

Used for headings and navigation elements. Selected for its clean, modern appearance and excellent readability.

Secondary Font: Open Sans

Used for body text and general content. Chosen for its versatility and clarity at various sizes.
